Welcome to the Waystar info page!

For those not in the know, Waystar is NOT a real company. It was an idea I had in 2023 during the early stages of a Roblox game I am making. It originally was just a simple logo. A four point star with the word 'Waystar' under it. The logo would go under many different iterations arriving at the tri color version of the "new-era" logo. While it may share a name with a healthcare payments coroporation and a HBO show, this is a totally new thing.
